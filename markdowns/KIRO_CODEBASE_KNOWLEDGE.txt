SEEDING
-------
Script: backend/seeding/seed.py
Run:    python seed.py --reset-demo   (from backend/seeding/ folder)

Key seeding functions:
- seed_appointments()               — 50 named demo appointments A1-A50
- seed_cross_appointments_for_reviews() — 22 completed appts between named customers
                                         and named providers, INTENTIONALLY unreviewed
                                         (so demo can show review submission flow)
- seed_recent_appointments_for_named_providers() — heavy 30-day seed, 3-7 appts/day
                                                    per named provider
- seed_provider_reviews()           — auto-seeds reviews for all EXCEPT [DEMO:CX_*] appts
- seed_service_selection()          — uses real ServiceOffering titles from DB
- run_heavy_seeding()               — 1000 customers × 1-5 random appointments

IMPORTANT SEEDING RULE:
  Appointments marked [DEMO:CX_*] are cross-appointments for the review demo flow.
  seed_provider_reviews() EXCLUDES these so they stay pending for customer to submit.
  Do NOT change this behaviour.
